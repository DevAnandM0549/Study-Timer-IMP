❓ How to Use StudyFlow

Master your productivity with our gamified Pomodoro timer

1

📝 Create Your Tasks

Go to the Tasks section and add what you need to accomplish. Set the duration, priority, and add a description to stay organized.

2

📚 Upload Study Materials

Click on any task to open its details. Upload PDFs, images, or text files that you need to study. These materials will be available during your focus sessions.

3

⏱️ Start Focus Sessions

Click "Start" on a task or go to the Focus Timer. Your study materials will appear in a side panel so you can study while the timer runs.

4

⭐ Earn XP & Level Up

Complete sessions and tasks to earn XP. Level up to unlock new titles and track your progress on the dashboard.

5

🏆 Unlock Achievements

Complete challenges like maintaining streaks, studying at specific times, or hitting milestones to unlock achievements and bonus XP.

6

🤖 Ask the AI Assistant

Need study advice? Click on "AI Assistant" to chat with our AI helper. Ask about study techniques, time management tips, motivation strategies, or any productivity questions!

7

📊 Track Your Analytics

View your study patterns, weekly activity, and session history in the Analytics section to understand your productivity trends.
